Tarte aux Citron

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 lemons
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Mix flour, butter, sugar, and egg yolk to form pastry dough
  3. Roll out dough and line a tart pan
  4. Bake crust for 10 minutes until lightly golden
  5. Zest and juice the lemons
  6. Whisk eggs with sugar until pale and fluffy
  7. Fold in lemon juice, zest, and melted butter
  8. Pour filling into pre-baked crust
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes until filling is set but slightly jiggly in center
  10. Cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  11. Dust with powdered sugar before serving
